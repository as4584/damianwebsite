FAQ

Common questions

How long does LLC formation take?

Most LLC formations are completed within 7-14 business days, depending on your state. Some states offer expedited processing for an additional fee.

What information do I need to get started?

You'll need your desired business name, business address, member information, and your state of formation. We'll guide you through everything else.

What states do you serve?

We can help you form your business in all 50 states. We also assist with multi-state registration for businesses expanding to new markets.

Do you provide legal or tax advice?

No. Innovation Business Development Solutions provides business formation, infrastructure, and operational support services. We do not provide legal advice, tax advice, or accounting services. We recommend consulting with qualified legal and tax professionals for advice specific to your situation.

How does the free consultation work?

We'll respond within 24 hours of your inquiry. The initial consultation is 30 minutes, free of charge, where we assess your needs and provide personalized service recommendations with clear pricing and no hidden fees.

Can you help with multi-state business expansion?

Yes. Multi-state coordination is one of our core capabilities. We handle foreign qualification filings, state-specific licensing, compliance monitoring, and ensure your infrastructure remains stable as you expand across state lines.
