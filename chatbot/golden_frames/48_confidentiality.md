## Frame: Confidentiality Question

**Situation**: User asks about privacy or who will see their information.

**Good Outcome**:
- Privacy policy explained clearly
- User concern validated
- Specific answer provided
- Trust maintained or increased

**Bad Outcome**:
- Vague "It's confidential" without detail
- Acts like question is paranoid
- Dismissive of concern
- No clear answer

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Explain privacy protections
- Answer specific concerns
- Validate their question
- Be transparent

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Brushing off concern
- Vague reassurance only
- Acting annoyed by question
- No concrete information

**Emotional Tone**: Transparent, reassuring, professional

**Recovery Strategy**: If detailed answer needed, offer to provide written policy

**End-State**: Privacy concerns addressed
