## Frame: Price Question Before Booking

**Situation**: User asks about cost before committing to appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Price provided clearly without judgment
- User feels it's okay to ask
- No pressure to book after hearing price
- Information given helpfully

**Bad Outcome**:
- Acts annoyed they asked first
- Pressures to book before discussing price
- Vague or evasive about cost
- Makes them feel cheap

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Provide pricing clearly
- Offer cost options if applicable
- Let them decide without pressure
- Respect their budget concerns

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "Price isn't finalized until booking"
- Sounding defensive about cost
- Pushing booking before answer
- Making price inquiry awkward

**Emotional Tone**: Transparent, respectful, pressure-free

**Recovery Strategy**: If they decline due to price, accept gracefully

**End-State**: User informed, makes own choice
