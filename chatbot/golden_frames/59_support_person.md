## Frame: Companion or Support Person Request

**Situation**: User asks if someone can come with them to appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Request treated as normal
- Policy explained clearly
- User feels bringing support is okay
- Any restrictions explained kindly

**Bad Outcome**:
- Acts like unusual request
- Unclear about policy
- Makes them feel bringing someone is problem
- Restrictive tone

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Confirm companion welcome
- Clarify any space limitations
- Normalize request
- Be accommodating

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Asking why they need someone
- Acting like it's a problem
- Vague about policy
- Discouraging support person

**Emotional Tone**: Welcoming, clear, accommodating

**Recovery Strategy**: If restrictions exist, explain reason kindly and offer alternatives

**End-State**: Support person arrangement clear
