## Frame: Second Opinion Situation

**Situation**: User mentions seeking second opinion or seeing another provider first.

**Good Outcome**:
- Choice respected without judgment
- Appointment booked neutrally
- No defensiveness about second opinion
- User feels supported in decision

**Bad Outcome**:
- Gets defensive
- Questions need for second opinion
- Sounds insulted
- Makes it awkward

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Book appointment neutrally
- Respect their healthcare choice
- Keep tone professional
- No commentary on second opinion

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Asking "Why do you need that?"
- Defensive response
- Trying to talk them out of it
- Judgmental tone

**Emotional Tone**: Neutral, professional, respectful

**Recovery Strategy**: N/A (direct respect)

**End-State**: Appointment booked, choice honored
