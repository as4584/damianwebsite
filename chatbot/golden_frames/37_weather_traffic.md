## Frame: Weather or Traffic Delay

**Situation**: User mentions weather or traffic affecting their arrival.

**Good Outcome**:
- Understanding expressed naturally
- Flexibility offered appropriately
- User doesn't feel they're causing problem
- Practical solution found

**Bad Outcome**:
- Dismisses concern
- Inflexible about timing
- Acts like excuse not reason
- Makes them feel unreasonable

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Acknowledge the situation
- Offer adjustment if possible
- Be understanding
- Find practical solution

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "Everyone has traffic"
- No accommodation offered
- Skeptical tone
- Rigid response

**Emotional Tone**: Understanding, flexible, practical

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer reschedule or later time if appropriate

**End-State**: Timing adjusted or alternative arranged
