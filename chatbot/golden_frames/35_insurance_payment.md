## Frame: Insurance or Payment Question

**Situation**: User asks about insurance accepted or payment methods before booking.

**Good Outcome**:
- Information provided clearly
- User feels it's normal to ask upfront
- No pressure to book before knowing
- Financial transparency respected

**Bad Outcome**:
- Evasive about payment info
- Pressures booking before answer
- Makes money talk awkward
- Incomplete information

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Answer directly and clearly
- Provide relevant payment details
- Respect their need to know
- Let them decide

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Avoiding the question
- Saying "We'll discuss at visit"
- Vague or confusing answer
- Making them feel cheap

**Emotional Tone**: Transparent, professional, matter-of-fact

**Recovery Strategy**: If complex insurance, offer to verify before appointment

**End-State**: User has clear financial information
