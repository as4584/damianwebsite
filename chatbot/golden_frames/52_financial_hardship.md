## Frame: Financial Hardship Mentioned

**Situation**: User indicates cost concern or financial difficulty.

**Good Outcome**:
- Concern taken seriously
- Payment options explored if available
- User doesn't feel judged for money concern
- Dignity fully preserved

**Bad Outcome**:
- Dismisses financial concern
- No options explored
- Makes them feel ashamed
- Pushes payment anyway

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Acknowledge concern respectfully
- Discuss payment options available
- Keep tone non-judgmental
- Offer what flexibility exists

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Minimizing concern
- Saying "You need to find a way"
- Judgmental about finances
- No accommodation attempt

**Emotional Tone**: Respectful, non-judgmental, dignified

**Recovery Strategy**: If no options available, provide information about assistance resources

**End-State**: Options explored, dignity maintained
