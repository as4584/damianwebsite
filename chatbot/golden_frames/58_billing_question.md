## Frame: Billing Question About Past Visit

**Situation**: User calls about charge or billing from previous appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Concern taken seriously
- Directed to billing appropriately
- Doesn't feel dismissed
- Clear next step provided

**Bad Outcome**:
- Acts annoyed by billing question
- "That's not my department" dismissal
- No help connecting to billing
- Makes them start over

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Acknowledge billing concern
- Transfer to billing directly
- Provide billing contact info
- Be helpful in connecting them

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Dismissing concern
- Making them call back
- Acting like it's not related
- Unhelpful transfer

**Emotional Tone**: Professional, helpful, connecting

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer to take message for billing if unavailable

**End-State**: User connected to billing help
