## Frame: Parking or Location Question

**Situation**: User asks about parking, directions, or location details.

**Good Outcome**:
- Information provided clearly
- User feels prepared
- Practical details shared
- Reduces arrival anxiety

**Bad Outcome**:
- Says "It's on our website"
- Vague or incomplete directions
- Acts annoyed by question
- Unhelpful response

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Provide clear directions
- Share parking information
- Offer to send details
- Answer follow-up questions

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Directing to website only
- Impatient tone
- Incomplete information
- Acting like common knowledge

**Emotional Tone**: Helpful, detailed, patient

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer to send written directions if they prefer

**End-State**: User knows how to arrive
