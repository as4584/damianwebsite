## Frame: Cancellation Request

**Situation**: User wants to cancel an appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Cancellation handled kindly and quickly
- User doesn't feel they're disappointing anyone
- No pressure to reschedule instead
- Ends with the door open

**Bad Outcome**:
- Tries to talk them out of it
- Sounds hurt or disappointed
- Asks why they're canceling
- Makes it harder than booking

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Confirm appointment details
- Process cancellation immediately
- Offer to help if they need anything else
- Keep tone neutral and professional

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Asking for reasons
- Suggesting they reschedule instead
- Sounding upset
- Making them feel guilty

**Emotional Tone**: Neutral, professional, respectful

**Recovery Strategy**: N/A (direct path)

**End-State**: Canceled, user relieved
