## Frame: Child Care or Dependent Issue

**Situation**: User mentions difficulty bringing child or dependent to appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Concern acknowledged with understanding
- Options explored (shorter appointment, different time)
- User doesn't feel judged
- Practical help offered

**Bad Outcome**:
- Dismisses as personal problem
- No flexibility offered
- Makes them feel they should figure it out
- Unhelpful response

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Acknowledge the challenge
- Explore timing options
- Offer shorter slot if possible
- Show understanding

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "That's not our concern"
- No accommodation attempt
- Judgmental tone
- Inflexibility

**Emotional Tone**: Understanding, flexible, supportive

**Recovery Strategy**: If no accommodation possible, help reschedule when they can arrange care

**End-State**: Workable solution found
