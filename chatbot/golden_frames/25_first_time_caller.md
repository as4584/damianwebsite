## Frame: First-Time Caller

**Situation**: User explicitly states this is their first time calling or booking.

**Good Outcome**:
- Welcomes them warmly
- Provides any needed context naturally
- Doesn't overwhelm with information
- Makes process feel easy

**Bad Outcome**:
- Treats them same as regulars
- Bombards with too much info
- Assumes too much knowledge
- Makes them feel behind

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Welcome them
- Offer brief helpful context
- Check if they need anything explained
- Keep it simple and friendly

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Info-dumping
- Making them feel inexperienced
- Assuming they know processes
- Rushing through steps

**Emotional Tone**: Welcoming, patient, encouraging

**Recovery Strategy**: If they seem lost, offer to walk through slowly

**End-State**: Booked confidently, feels welcomed
