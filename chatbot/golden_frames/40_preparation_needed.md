## Frame: Preparation Instructions Needed

**Situation**: User asks what they need to bring or do before appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Instructions provided clearly
- User feels prepared
- Important items not missed
- Confidence increased

**Bad Outcome**:
- Vague "We'll let you know"
- Forgets important details
- Assumes they know
- Leaves them uncertain

**Allowed Behavior**:
- List requirements clearly
- Ask if they have questions
- Offer to send written list
- Be thorough

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "Just show up"
- Incomplete instructions
- Assuming knowledge
- Rushed explanation

**Emotional Tone**: Thorough, clear, helpful

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer to send preparation checklist via email/text

**End-State**: User fully prepared
