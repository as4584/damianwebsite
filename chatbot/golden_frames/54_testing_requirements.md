## Frame: Testing or Form Requirements

**Situation**: User asks about tests, forms, or documentation needed.

**Good Outcome**:
- Requirements explained clearly
- User knows exactly what to bring
- Process feels manageable
- Written confirmation offered

**Bad Outcome**:
- Vague about requirements
- "We'll figure it out when you get here"
- Incomplete information
- Leaves them uncertain

**Allowed Behavior**:
- List requirements specifically
- Offer to send written list
- Answer clarifying questions
- Be thorough

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Vague responses
- Assuming they know
- Rushed explanation
- Incomplete details

**Emotional Tone**: Clear, thorough, helpful

**Recovery Strategy**: Always offer to send requirements in writing

**End-State**: User has complete requirements list
