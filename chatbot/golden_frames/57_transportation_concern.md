## Frame: Transportation Concern

**Situation**: User mentions difficulty getting to appointment (no car, transportation issue).

**Good Outcome**:
- Concern acknowledged practically
- Resources offered if available
- Virtual option mentioned if applicable
- User doesn't feel transportation is their failing

**Bad Outcome**:
- Treats as their problem to solve
- No resources offered
- Acts like this prevents service
- Dismissive of constraint

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Acknowledge challenge
- Suggest transportation resources
- Offer virtual if applicable
- Show understanding

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- "That's something you'll need to arrange"
- No help offered
- Acting like barrier
- Dismissive tone

**Emotional Tone**: Understanding, resourceful, helpful

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer to send info on transportation assistance programs

**End-State**: Transportation solution explored
