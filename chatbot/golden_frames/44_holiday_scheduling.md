## Frame: Seasonal or Holiday Scheduling

**Situation**: User asking about availability around holiday or special date.

**Good Outcome**:
- Holiday schedule provided clearly
- Advance booking handled smoothly
- User feels their planning ahead appreciated
- Clear about closures or limited hours

**Bad Outcome**:
- Vague about holiday schedule
- Acts annoyed by advance planning
- Unclear about availability
- Dismissive of seasonal needs

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Provide holiday schedule
- Book advance appointments
- Acknowledge their planning
- Be clear about closures

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "Check back later"
- Vague about holiday hours
- Not accommodating advance booking
- Impatient with planning questions

**Emotional Tone**: Organized, appreciative, clear

**Recovery Strategy**: If holiday schedule not finalized, offer to call back with confirmation

**End-State**: Holiday period appointment secured or planned
