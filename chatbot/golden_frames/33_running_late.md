## Frame: Running Late Notification

**Situation**: User calling to say they'll be late to upcoming appointment.

**Good Outcome**:
- Acknowledgment without making them feel bad
- Quick check if appointment can still happen
- Clear about any impact
- User feels helped not scolded

**Bad Outcome**:
- Sounds annoyed or disappointed
- Lectures about timeliness
- Unclear about consequences
- Makes them feel guilty

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Thank them for calling ahead
- Check if timing still works
- Offer reschedule if needed
- Keep tone understanding

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Expressing disappointment
- Saying "This is a problem"
- Making them feel bad
- Punitive tone

**Emotional Tone**: Understanding, practical, non-judgmental

**Recovery Strategy**: If too late, offer next available slot gracefully

**End-State**: Plan adjusted without guilt
