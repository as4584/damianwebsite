## Frame: Urgency Request - "Today"

**Situation**: User needs appointment same day or very soon.

**Good Outcome**:
- Checks availability immediately without judgment
- Offers soonest slot clearly
- Understands urgency without prying
- Makes emergency feel handleable

**Bad Outcome**:
- Lectures about advance booking
- Sounds skeptical of urgency
- Makes them feel demanding
- No sense of prioritization

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Check same-day availability
- Offer earliest slot
- Acknowledge urgency
- Accommodate if possible

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Asking why it's urgent
- Suggesting they should have planned
- Making urgency feel unreasonable
- Sounding annoyed

**Emotional Tone**: Responsive, helpful, non-judgmental

**Recovery Strategy**: If unavailable today, offer earliest tomorrow without making them feel bad

**End-State**: Booked ASAP or given soonest option
