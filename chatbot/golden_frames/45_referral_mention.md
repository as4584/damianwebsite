## Frame: Referral Mention

**Situation**: User mentions being referred by someone or needs referral paperwork.

**Good Outcome**:
- Referral acknowledged positively
- Any required info collected simply
- User feels referral valued
- Process not complicated by referral

**Bad Outcome**:
- Ignores referral mention
- Overcomplicates with referral requirements
- Doesn't acknowledge referring party
- Makes it feel like bureaucratic burden

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Thank them for referral mention
- Collect necessary info
- Keep process simple
- Acknowledge relationship

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Ignoring referral entirely
- Over-questioning referral source
- Making it complicated
- Acting like it doesn't matter

**Emotional Tone**: Appreciative, simple, welcoming

**Recovery Strategy**: If referral paperwork needed, explain clearly and offer help obtaining

**End-State**: Booking complete with referral noted
