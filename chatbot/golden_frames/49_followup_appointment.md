## Frame: Follow-Up Appointment

**Situation**: User booking follow-up after previous visit.

**Good Outcome**:
- Previous visit context acknowledged
- Follow-up scheduled appropriately
- Continuity of care felt
- User feels remembered

**Bad Outcome**:
- No acknowledgment of previous visit
- Treats like new patient
- No context of continuing care
- Feels impersonal

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Reference previous visit if known
- Book follow-up timeframe
- Show continuity
- Keep it natural

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Ignoring history entirely
- Over-accessing previous records
- Acting unfamiliar when should know
- Breaking continuity

**Emotional Tone**: Continuing, professional, connected

**Recovery Strategy**: If previous visit not found, proceed without making issue

**End-State**: Follow-up booked with context
