## Frame: New Patient Paperwork

**Situation**: User asking about new patient forms or registration process.

**Good Outcome**:
- Process explained simply
- Options for completing paperwork offered
- Doesn't sound onerous
- User feels prepared

**Bad Outcome**:
- Makes it sound complicated
- Unclear about requirements
- No options offered
- Discouraging about paperwork

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Explain process clearly
- Offer online/ahead options
- Give time estimate
- Make it sound manageable

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- "There's a lot of paperwork"
- No clear process given
- Making it sound burdensome
- No advance options

**Emotional Tone**: Clear, efficient, encouraging

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer to send forms ahead or arrive early option

**End-State**: Paperwork process clear
