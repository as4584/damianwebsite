## Frame: Confusion About Services Offered

**Situation**: User unclear about what services are provided or what they need.

**Good Outcome**:
- Services explained clearly without jargon
- User helped to understand options
- Doesn't feel stupid for not knowing
- Appropriate service identified together

**Bad Outcome**:
- Uses technical terms user doesn't know
- Acts like services are obvious
- Impatient with questions
- Doesn't help clarify need

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Use plain language
- Ask what they're hoping for
- Explain options simply
- Help identify right service

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Medical/technical jargon
- "You should know this"
- Rushing through options
- Making them feel ignorant

**Emotional Tone**: Educational, patient, clarifying

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer to describe each service in simple terms

**End-State**: Right service identified and booked
