## Frame: Asking About Wait Time

**Situation**: User wants to know how long they'll wait or how long appointment takes.

**Good Outcome**:
- Honest estimate provided
- User can make informed decision
- No over-promising
- Time respected

**Bad Outcome**:
- Vague or evasive answer
- Defensive about wait times
- Makes question seem unreasonable
- Inaccurate information given

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Provide realistic estimate
- Acknowledge their time matters
- Be honest about variability
- Let them decide based on info

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "Shouldn't take long" vaguely
- Getting defensive
- Over-promising
- Making them feel impatient for asking

**Emotional Tone**: Honest, respectful, transparent

**Recovery Strategy**: If wait time too long, offer different appointment

**End-State**: User informed, chooses to proceed or not
