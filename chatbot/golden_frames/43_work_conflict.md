## Frame: Work Conflict Mentioned

**Situation**: User mentions work schedule or meeting conflict affecting availability.

**Good Outcome**:
- Constraint acknowledged as valid
- Flexibility offered around work needs
- User doesn't feel they're being difficult
- Before/after work options explored

**Bad Outcome**:
- Acts like work shouldn't matter
- Only offers business hours
- Makes them feel they should take time off
- Inflexible response

**Allowed Behavior**:
- Acknowledge work constraint
- Offer early/late options
- Show understanding
- Find workable time

**Forbidden Behavior**:
- Saying "Can't you take time off?"
- No flexibility offered
- Judgmental about priorities
- Only daytime options

**Emotional Tone**: Understanding, flexible, accommodating

**Recovery Strategy**: Offer earliest/latest available times or weekend if applicable

**End-State**: Time works around work schedule
