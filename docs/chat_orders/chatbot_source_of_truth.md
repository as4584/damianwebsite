Mission & Intent

Our mission is to guide people calmly and kindly through a short intake so a human specialist can follow up. We provide a human-centered, empathetic experience that respects the user's time and emotional state while collecting the necessary information to move forward.

This chatbot exists to:
- Guide people through an 8-step intake process
- Calmly acknowledge and answer user questions
- Collect accurate intake information (Name, Email, Business Type, etc.)
- Prepare qualified leads for human follow-up
- Ensure users feel understood and respected

Business Entity Structures (High-Level Only)

The following entity structures exist in the United States. Availability, requirements, and suitability vary by state and circumstance.

Individual & Unincorporated Structures

Sole Proprietorship

Sole Proprietorship with DBA

Independent Contractor (tax status, not a legal entity)

These are typically used for simplicity and speed, but do not create separation between the individual and the business.

Partnerships

General Partnership (GP)

Limited Partnership (LP)

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Limited Liability Limited Partnership (LLLP)

These are used when multiple people operate a business together, with varying degrees of liability and management roles.

Limited Liability Companies (LLCs)

Single-Member LLC

Multi-Member LLC

Member-Managed LLC

Manager-Managed LLC

Professional LLC (PLLC)

Series LLC (where allowed)

Low-Profit LLC (L3C)

Anonymous LLC (state-specific)

LLCs are commonly used for flexibility and liability protection, but details matter.

Corporations (For-Profit)

C Corporation

S Corporation (tax election, not a separate entity type)

Professional Corporation (PC / PSC)

Close Corporation

Corporations are often used for scaling, investment, or formal governance.

Nonprofit & Mission-Driven Entities

Nonprofit Corporation

Public Charity (501(c)(3))

Private Foundation

Religious Corporation

Other 501(c) organizations

These entities are formed for public or member benefit rather than private profit.

Cooperatives & Member-Owned Entities

Cooperative (Co-Op)

Worker Cooperative

Consumer Cooperative

Credit Union

Mutual organizations

These entities prioritize member ownership and shared benefit.

Trust-Based & Specialized Structures

Business Trust

Statutory Trust

Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT)

Holding Companies

Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs)

These structures are typically used for asset separation or specialized use cases.

Important Clarity Notes

An S-Corporation is a tax election, not a new legal entity.

Many structures overlap legally but differ by state statute, tax treatment, or licensing.

Some entities are available only in certain states.

Suitability depends on ownership, activity, location, and goals.

What Consultations Are For

Consultations are used to:

confirm the correct structure

review ownership and operations

The chatbot does not finalize entity selection.
